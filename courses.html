Engineering & Technology

Include Civil, Mechanical, CSE, Electrical, Aerospace, AI/ML, and more

Gain practical skills and technical expertise across high-demand disciplines like Civil, Mechanical, CSE, Electrical, Aerospace, and AI/ML. Curriculum blends classroom learning with hands-on labs, industry projects, and real-world problem-solving. Launch your career with guidance from IIT/NIT-experienced faculty and top-notch infrastructure.

Management & Commerce

Courses Offered: BBA, MBA, B.Com – Specializations in Finance, Marketing, HR & Business Analytics.

Step into the world of business with programs designed to build strategic thinking, leadership, and data-driven decision-making. Gain real-world exposure through case studies, internships, live projects, and expert-led sessions.

Agriculture & Horticulture

Courses Offered: B.Sc Agriculture, B.Sc Horticulture – Practical training in modern farming, agribusiness & sustainability.

Explore the science of soil, crops, irrigation, and plant health with hands-on learning and field exposure. Designed to equip students with skills for smart farming, agri-entrepreneurship, and sustainable rural development.
